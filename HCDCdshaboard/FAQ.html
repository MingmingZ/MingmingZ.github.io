What is HCDC dashboard?

HCDC dashboard is a free online tool to get neighborhood information. Our interactive map presents data in a visually pleasing and easy-to-understand format. You can Map one indicator for Harris County at neighborhood level or get the Community Profile to get all indicators for one neighborhood.
